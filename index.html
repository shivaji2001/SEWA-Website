About Us

We are a not-for-profit organisation that ensures empowerment for poor children. Our team has been working towards creating happier and healthier childhoods. We aim to provide help to the children for their education, health & nutrition,clothes and other accessories. We ensure this by providing the funds to the needy ones and also providing them information about the various scholarship programmes and schemes initiated by the government to help the poor children.
